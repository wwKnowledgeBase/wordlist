bigram

bigram, a list of two letter words. Once you start a thing like this, it's hard to know when to stop. Some of these words dwell in the shadowy netherlands of English, only called into existence when a really intense Scrabble game has gone into overtime, or when an overeducated paranoid believes it almost possible to translate babytalk. A few of the words that appear only in Chambers Dictionary are a little hard to swallow, but that dictionary has some canonical sanction in International Scrabble circles.
